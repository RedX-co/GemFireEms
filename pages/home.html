Fire


Gem County Fire-EMS maintains three fire stations; one in Emmett built in 1983, one in Letha built in 2002, and one at the intersection of Butte road and Van Deusen road built in 2010. The stations are manned by 2 full time employees and over 30 trained volunteer firefighters who are from various backgrounds from farmers to executives. The Fire District has 3 engines, 3 water tenders, 3 light brush trucks, 2 heavy brush trucks, 1 ladder truck, 1 extrication/rescue truck, and 2 command vehicle.
