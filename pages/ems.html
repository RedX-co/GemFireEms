EMS District information

Gem County Fire-EMS provides EMS service for the 566 square miles of Gem County. As of the 2010 census, the population of our service area was 16,719.

Frequently Asked Questions

Billing questions/issues

Gem County Fire-EMS contracts a third party billing service. Please contact Systems Design for all billing questions. Systems Design 1-800-238-9398

How do I contract an ambulance for an event?

Gem County Fire-EMS offers multiple options and prices to contract EMS personnel and equipment for your upcoming event.

  • DEDICATED EMS AMBULANCE with 2 medical staff $125 an hour. (Minimum 2 hours)
  • DEDICATED QRU with 1 medical staff $60 an hour. (Minimum 2 hours)
  • NON-DEDICATED EMS AMBULANCE with 2 medical staff $25 an hour. (Minimum 2 hours)
