Mission Statement

To provide protecton of life and property for Gem County Fire Protection District #1 by providing a fire protection service to prevent, surpress, and control fire and other emergencies.

Fire Protection Area

Gem County Fire-EMS provides fire suppression and rescue services to residents of Gem County Fire Protection District #1. We also provide services in surrounding areas through mutual aid agreements and requests.
