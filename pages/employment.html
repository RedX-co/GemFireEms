Volunteer Firefighter

Opening: Volunteer Firefighter - Continuosly recruiting - No Closing date

Description: Volunteer Firefighters are dedicated members of the fire department committed to servicing their community. Gem County Fire-EMS Volunteers spend extensive hours of training to become qualified to respond in a safe and professional manner to emergencies within the fire district, Volunteers are encourage to come to emergency calls 24/7 when called upon and available. Volunteer firefighters are required to attend fire drills twice a month for a total of 8 hrs minimum per month. Volunteers are expected to maintain any training requirements set forth by the department.

Requirements:

  • 18 years of Age
  • Valid Driver License
  • Must be able to complete physical agility test and pack test yearly
  • Live within Gem County Fire Protection District #1

PRN EMT/Paramedic

Opening: PRN EMT/Paramedic - Continuosly recruiting - No Closing date

Description: PRN EMT’s and Paramedics are dedicated members of the Fire-EMS department committed to servicing their community. Gem County Fire-EMS personal spend extensive hours of training to become qualified to respond in a safe and professional manner to emergencies within the Fire-EMS district. PRN employees are required to work 12 hour shifts monthly to maintain competency, and are also encouraged to on call time. PRN EMT’s and Paramedics are required to attend trainings twice a month for a total of 8 hrs minimum per month. Employees are expected to maintain any training requirements set forth by the department.

Requirements:

  • 18 years of Age
  • Valid Driver License
  • Valid National registry or State of Idaho EMS license
  • Must be able to complete physical agility test and pack test yearly
  • Live within Gem County
