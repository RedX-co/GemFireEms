Fire


The Fire District maintains two (3) fire stations; one in Emmett, built in 1983 and one in Letha, built in 2002, and one in the Squaw Butte area. The stations are manned by38 trained volunteer firefighters who are from various backgrounds from farmers to executives. These firefighters are paid per call then hourly The Fire Distict has 5 engines, 3 water tenders, 1 ladder truck, 3 brush trucks, 1 rescue squad, 1 station vehicle and 1 commad vehicle
